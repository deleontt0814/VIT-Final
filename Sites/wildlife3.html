Gulls or seagulls are seabirds of the family Laridae in the suborder Lari.
They are most closely related to the terns (family Sternidae) and only distantly
related to auks, skimmers, and more distantly to the waders.
Until the 21st century, most gulls were placed in the genus Larus,
but this arrangement is now considered polyphyletic, leading to the
resurrection of several genera.
